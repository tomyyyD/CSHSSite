Service Hours

Service hours are how we will track your participation in the community.

Requirements

Each member is required to give at least 10 service hours per year.

Information

At least 5 of your hours must be computer science related. To get computer science related hours, you can go to CSHS meetings, attend hackathons/coding competitions with Coding Crew, be a CS tutor, or volunteer in an outside organize in the CS field.
